# Manhattan Vacation Rental Market

## Project Description
Analyzing Manhattan Airbnb data to see which neighborhoods and property sizes were more attractive for vacation rentals

## Tools Used
- Google Sheets

## Key Findings
- It highlights the top 10 most popular neighborhoods in Manhattan for vacation rentals, revealing key areas of opportunity for investment.
- This analysis also examines the most in-demand property sizes among renters, along with their associated daily, monthly, and annualy revenue potential.
- In Manhattan, one-bedroom listings are the most popular, followed by two-bedroom listings, with studios (0 bedrooms) ranking third.
- Except for Midtown which reflects studios as the most popular property size, the rest of top listings show one bedroom listings as the most popular property size
- The recommendation is to invest in the 3 highest-performing neighborhoods of Manhattan to obtain a high revenue
- The 3 best neighborhoods to invest in are: Lower East Side, Hells Kitchen and Harlem.
